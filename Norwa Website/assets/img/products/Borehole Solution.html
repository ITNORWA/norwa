Borehole Rehabilitation

Our technicians handle the installation of all types of borehole pumps, with expertise in submersible pump handling, we offer safe and efficient lifting and lowering services. Routine maintenance is essential for the longevity and perfomance of your borehole. Our maintenance service include thorough diagnostic and flushing to clear any blockages. Regular maintenance checks by our team help identify and resolve issues early, reducing downtime and extending the lifespan of your borehole.
