Mobile R.O

Mobile Reverse Osmosis Desalination plant

This unit is built to move, literally and mission wise. Its designed to deliver clean safe water anywhere its needed. The unit is designed to be mounted on a truck or trailer, making it easy to transport to remote locations or disaster-stricken areas. It can produce up to 40,000 liters of clean water per day, depending on the salinity of the source water.
