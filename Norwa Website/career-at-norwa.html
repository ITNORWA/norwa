At Norwa Africa, working to serve the environment is our day-to-day reality. In our water, pumps and solar businesses, our teams take action on the ground and help find solutions to shape the future. To support them in the work they do, our human resources policy helps them build their skills but also achieve fulfilment in a positive, inclusive working environment. We also raise employees’ awareness of the Norwa group’s social commitments and we place particular importance on compliance with health and safety rules, which is our number one priority.

Modern Campus Facilities

Throughout your career with Norwa, our aim is to give you numerous opportunities to develop and achieve fulfilment. By joining us, you will be able to build a multi-faceted career and realise your talents at every stage!

Our people are our greatest asset, and they can take part in a broad array of training opportunities throughout their careers. Regardless of your background or current role, you are at the heart of our training efforts. Self-study, online learning and in-person sessions: there are a number of options.

We strongly encourage people to move between roles within the Group, and internal candidates account for over a third of the positions we fill. This allows our employees to: Build skills in different roles, Gain experience of our numerous business models and markets, Develop an international career, with roles available across East Africa countries, Forge an exciting career working across the Group

Our collective success is built on our core values and behaviours. Those values are displayed every day by all our people. They guide their behaviour and reflect the mindset with which we address the environmental and social challenges of our time.
